Flooring

Check our Products

Wood flooring is a classical look that has transcended centuries of style.It is natural,ecofriendly and incredibly durable hence has been used for floor construction for ages.

A solid wood floor can last for hundreds and hundreds of years.We make the most durable wood floors using the best wood available- Teak.

  • By using teak which has high oil content,it is decay resistance.
  • The floors when finished are aesthetically appealing and the attention to detail is impeccable.
  • The wood is treated to ensure that it can't be attacked by insects.

Teak has a high quality of natural oils that are responsible for the outstanding resistance of teak wood to harsh conditions,wear and tear.

For the wood,After it is cut,meticulous planing is done on all four sides to smoothen saw marks and level the planks.For T $ G wood flooring plan,a machine cuts the edges to make the boards fit tightly together. Should you want an older antique look,the planks are passed through a distressing process to give them the antique look.The final product is sealed/stained with several coats of protective finish.The wood planks are sanded,polished and finished.Through the whole process,our staff does routine quality checks through the entire flooring process to make sure the work is well done.

Types

T$G

It is an easy way to install because floorboards are all fitted with protruding(tongue) and concave(groove) parts to fit together like puzzle pieces.You would simply fit the tongues and grooves together for a tight and snug fit.This brings about perfect joinery.

Wood Block

This is a particular profile of flooring that consists of individual blocks of flooring installed to provide a geometrical pattern.As the parquet blocks can be laid in a variety of patterns, they offer flexibility and the chance to create a unique look to suit and complement your property. Once the unfinished blocks have been laid, they must be sanded, can be stained if desired, and must be protected with layers of lacquer or varnish.
