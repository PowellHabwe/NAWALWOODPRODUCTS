Wood flooring is a classical look that has transcended centuries of style.It is natural,ecofriendly and incredibly durable hence has been used for floor construction for ages.

A solid wood floor can be expected to last for hundreds of years. We create the most durable wood floors possible by using the best wood availableâ€”Teak.

Teak has a high quality of natural oils that are responsible for the outstanding resistance of teak wood to harsh conditions,wear and tear.

After the wood is cut, it is meticulously planed on all four sides to smooth out saw marks and level the planks. A machine cuts the edges of T $ G wood flooring plan to make the boards fit tightly together. If you want an older antique look, the planks go through a distressing process to achieve that look. Several coats of protective finish are applied to the finished product. The planks of wood have been sanded, polished, and finished. Throughout the flooring process, our staff performs routine quality checks to ensure that the work is done correctly.

Types

T$G

It is a simple method of installation because all floorboards have protruding (tongue) and concave (groove) parts that fit together like puzzle pieces. Simply fit the tongues and grooves together to create a tight and snug fit. This results in flawless joinery.

Wood Block

This is a type of flooring profile that consists of individual blocks of flooring installed in a geometrical pattern. Because parquet blocks can be laid in a variety of patterns, they provide flexibility and the opportunity to create a one-of-a-kind look that will suit and complement your property. After the unfinished blocks have been laid, they must be sanded, stained if desired, and protected with layers of lacquer or varnish.
